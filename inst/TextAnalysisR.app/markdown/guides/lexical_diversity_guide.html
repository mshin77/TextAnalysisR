Lexical Diversity Metrics

Lexical diversity measures vocabulary richness and lexical sophistication by quantifying the relationship between unique words (types) and total words (tokens) in a text.

Metrics

  • TTR: Unique words / total words (sensitive to length)
  • MTLD: Length-robust sequential analysis
  • MATTR: Moving average TTR (reduces length bias)
  • CTTR: Length-corrected TTR

Also: Herdan's C, Summer's S, Maas, Dugast's U, Yule's K

Usage

Higher values indicate richer vocabulary. Use MTLD or MATTR for length-robust analysis.
